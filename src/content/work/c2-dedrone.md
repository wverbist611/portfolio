## Overview

C2 is Dedrone's flagship command-and-control product, designed to protect critical airspaces from aerial threats — primarily unauthorized drones. Built for state, local, and defense operators, C2 needed to deliver real-time situational awareness and decisive action capability in high-stakes environments where seconds matter and cognitive load must be minimal.

As the sole designer on this project, I was responsible for the end-to-end experience — from product strategy and interaction design through to a fully documented design system.

## The Challenge

Airspace security operators face a uniquely demanding set of conditions. They must continuously monitor a live environment populated by multiple simultaneous aerial tracks, distinguish between friendly, neutral, and hostile assets, and respond to threats quickly and with confidence.

The core design challenge: how do you present complex, dynamic airspace data in a way that supports fast, accurate decision-making without overwhelming the operator?

## Approach

### Symbology and Classification

A foundational design decision was the development of a clear visual language for track classification. Every aerial track in C2 is assigned a classification — Hostile, Unknown, Neutral, Friendly, or Owned — each represented through a consistent symbology system designed to communicate threat level at a glance.

### The Monitoring Panel

The Monitoring panel serves as the operator's primary awareness surface. It organizes all detected tracks — Unmanned, Manned, and Undetermined — into structured, independently scrollable containers with clear hierarchy.

### Automated Mitigation

When a compatible mitigator is available and in range, C2 surfaces a recommended response directly within the threat card — allowing the operator to engage with a single action.

## Design System

To support the complexity and scale of C2, I defined and built a comprehensive design system from the ground up. Every component, state, and interaction pattern was designed to be reusable, scalable, and rigorously specced for engineering handoff. The system was adopted across Dedrone's full product portfolio.

## Outcome

C2 is now Dedrone's flagship product experience, deployed with state, local, and defense customers.
