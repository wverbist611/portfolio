## Overview

As a Principal UX Designer at Amazon, I defined and led design on some of the company's most nascent and ambitious technologies — including Just Walk Out, the experience behind Amazon Go, and Astro, Amazon's consumer robot.

## Role

Working across multiple lines of business, I served as a Customer Experience Bar Raiser (CXBR), helping teams reframe and define new opportunities from the customer's perspective. I also functioned as a design bar raiser supporting hiring across the organization, and mentored junior designers to help grow the broader design community at Amazon.
